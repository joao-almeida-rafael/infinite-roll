How are we

 Infinite Roll is a board game store with space and a community of players for you to enjoy. In here we play role playing games, trading card games, war games and boardgames.

 Infinite Roll was born because we felt the need for a place like this in our city, where people could enjoy play, trade and talk about board games with other.

Our space is big with large tables and plenty of chairs, good geeky environment with coffee and snacks!

 We were established in 2017 and we have made strong partnerships with many big and small companies in the industry, so we could provide you with the best products, from games to collectibles.

 Our mission is to grow our presence in the world of board gaming so we can bring more and better events to our space and enlarge our community of players.
